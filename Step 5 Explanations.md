# **Explanations for step 5** 







**| HTML Tag             | Purpose                                                           |**

**| -------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------- |**

**| `<img>`              | Displays an image on the webpage.                                 |**

**| `src`                | Specifies the location (path) of the image, audio, or video file. |**

**| `alt`                | Displays alternative text if the image cannot be loaded.          |**

**| `<audio>`            | Embeds an audio file.                                             |**

**| `controls`           | Adds play, pause, volume, and progress controls.                  |**

**| `<source>`           | Specifies the media file and its type.                            |**

**| `<video>`            | Embeds a video file.                                              |**

**| `width` and `height` | Set the size of the image or video.                               |**
