# **Objective for practical 4** 







**HTML form objects used:**

**<form> – Creates the form**

**<input type="text"> – Employee ID, Name**

**<input type="email"> – Email address**

**<input type="password"> – Password**

**<input type="tel"> – Phone number**

**<input type="date"> – Date of birth**

**<input type="radio"> – Gender selection**

**<select> and <option> – Department dropdown**

**<input type="checkbox"> – Skills**

**<textarea> – Address**

**<input type="file"> – Resume upload**

**<input type="submit"> – Submit the form**

**<input type="reset"> – Clear the form**
